# Game Ideas for Brain Busters

Client-side browser games that can be added to the site.

## Classic Puzzles
1. Nonogram (Picross) - reveal pictures by filling cells based on number clues
2. Kakuro - cross-sum puzzles combining crosswords and Sudoku
3. Futoshiki - inequality Sudoku with greater-than/less-than constraints
4. Killer Sudoku - Sudoku with cage sum constraints
5. Hitori - eliminate duplicates in rows/columns by shading cells
6. Nurikabe - create islands of specific sizes by shading cells
7. Slitherlink - draw a loop around cells following number clues
8. Light Up (Akari) - place lights to illuminate entire grid
9. Hashiwokakero (Bridges) - connect islands with bridges
10. Masyu - draw a loop through pearls following rules
11. Shikaku - divide grid into rectangles matching number clues
12. Fillomino - fill regions with numbers matching their area
13. Tentai Show (Spiral Galaxies) - divide grid around center points
14. Numberlink - connect matching numbers without crossing paths
15. Ripple Effect - fill regions where same numbers have specific spacing

24. Cryptogram - decode substitution cipher to reveal quote

27. Word Wheel - make words from letters around a wheel
28. Text Twist - find all words from given letters
29. Letterpress - claim letters by making words
30. Ghost - add letters without completing a word
