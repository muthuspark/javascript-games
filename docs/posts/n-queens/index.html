N-Queens Puzzle - Chess Logic Game

Place N queens on an NxN chessboard so no two queens threaten each other. Classic chess puzzle from 4x4 to 12x12.

PuzzlesChessLogic Games

How to Play

Place N queens on an NxN chess board where no two queens threaten each other.

  1. Click a cell to place or remove a queen
  2. Queens cannot share the same row, column, or diagonal
  3. Threatened squares will be highlighted in red
  4. Place all queens without conflicts to win!

Frequently Asked Questions

Place N queens on an NxN chessboard so no two queens can attack each other. Queens attack horizontally, vertically, and diagonally.

The 8 Queens puzzle has 92 distinct solutions, or 12 fundamental solutions when accounting for symmetry.
